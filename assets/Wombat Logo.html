W-Wave

One stroke does three jobs at once: it spells a W, it draws a position-over-time waveform — the heart of a funscript — and it forms a wombat's brow, eyes and nose. Coral picks up at the in-point and resolves to teal at the out-point.

Built on a single rounded stroke, so it stays crisp and recognisable all the way down to a 16px favicon.
